Some projects I have recently worked on:

BococaCampFinder

BococaCampFinder is a web app that helps you find the perfect summer camp for your child in the BoCoCa Neighborhoods of Brooklyn and surrounding areas. BococaCampFinder was created after talking with fellow mom friends and realizing the lack of a central place to find all the summer camps within their neighborhood.

BococaCampFinder - Repo

Weather Finder App

This is a simple weather app built using the OpenWeatherMap API. You can search by city name or city and country. Each city results page background changes based on the actual current weather happening now in the city you selected.

Weather Finder App - Repo
